Class Project Timeline/Walkthrough

June 2016

FIRST STEPS

I started with the content map and wireframe. This led to me doing research on what framework I wanted to use. I stumbled upon bootstrap, and the bootflat UI kit. From there I spent significant time on designing a template that I would build the rest of my website on.

BUILDING PAGES

From there, I spent time building the other pages and laying out the footings for completing many of the other requirements. At this point the web font, favicon, and icons were chosen

July 2016

MAJOR REQUIREMENTS

On the work page, many of the project requirements were added. Canvas, audio, video, server-side scripting, client-side scripting, Authentication, JSON, persistent storage, and contact forms were created.

FINISHING TOUCHES

The about page was finished, final touches on CSS and HTML layouts and look and feel of website. Final testing of links also done.
